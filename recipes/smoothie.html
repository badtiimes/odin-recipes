banana oat smoothie

description

a great way to start your morning!
i do this when i'm lazy to cook a real breakfast.

ingredients

for 1 serving
  • ½ cup rolled oats (50 g)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk (240 ml), of choice

steps

  1. add rolled oats to a blender and blend until the oats are the size of a fine crumb.
  2. add the banana and milk and blend well.
  3. pour in a glass.
  4. enjoy!
